Empowering Instructors, Elevating Learning

Transcribe

A powerful application designed to accurately capture spoken input in multiple languages. It enables users to record audio, generate high-accuracy transcriptions, and download both audio and transcription documents. Perfect for educators, this tool streamlines the creation of speaking activities and enhances classroom engagement.

Overview

A transcription tool built for educators and students, enabling seamless audio recording, transcription, and document export. Designed to enhance classroom engagement through interactive activities and effective content creation.

Key Highlights

- Supports transcription in over five languages with exceptional accuracy.
- Simplifies the process of recording audio and exporting documents.
- User-friendly interface tailored for educational settings.

Audience

Educators and language instructors looking for tools to create engaging speaking and listening exercises for students.

Responsibilities

- Designed an intuitive interface to ensure ease of use.
- Implemented multi-language transcription capabilities.
- Developed export functionality for audio and transcription files.

Tools Used

Python, JavaScript, Google Speech-to-Text API, and Flask for back-end integration.

Full Development

Created the application from concept to deployment, including user interface design, back-end integration, and performance optimization for seamless transcription and audio recording.

Results

- Improved classroom engagement through interactive activities.
- Reduced preparation time for educators by automating transcription tasks.
- Positive feedback from users on accuracy and ease of use.

Takeaways

- Prioritize simplicity and usability for diverse audiences.
- Multi-language support enhances accessibility and global reach.
- Combining automation with educational tools saves time and boosts productivity.
