Driving Results Through Microlearning

Boosting Productivity through Microlearning

I created a 3-minute microlearning course featuring a clear, engaging, and visually intuitive learning experience. Built with Rise and guided by Bloom's Taxonomy, it uses infographics to provide quick, actionable insights.

Overview

This microlearning course focuses on delivering key insights in under three minutes. It combines effective design, practical examples, and impactful visuals for a seamless learning experience.

Key Highlights

- A minimalist, clean design for effortless navigation.
- Infographics to simplify complex concepts.
- Guided by Bloom's Taxonomy for measurable learning outcomes.

Audience

Professionals and teams needing fast, focused training for enhanced productivity and actionable insights.

Responsibilities

- Developed a 3-step course with intuitive flow.
- Designed visuals to maximize engagement in a brief format.
- Applied learning theories to structure meaningful content.

Tools Used

Articulate Rise, Adobe Illustrator, Bloom's Taxonomy framework.

Full Development

Created an end-to-end learning solution by structuring content, designing infographics, and implementing responsive layouts for quick and effective training.

Results

- Enhanced learner retention with concise, impactful content.
- Positive feedback on usability and engagement.
- Increased adoption of microlearning solutions across teams.

Takeaways

- Keep content concise and focused on actionable insights.
- Use visuals strategically to support learner comprehension.
- Prioritize clarity and usability for busy professionals.
